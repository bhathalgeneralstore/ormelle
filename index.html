Bhathal General Store

Welcome to Bhathal Indian Store, your friendly neighborhood Indian grocery store! Nestled in the heart of Ormelle , Treviso, we offer a warm and inviting space filled with the rich aromas and vibrant colors of Indian cuisine. Our store is a treasure trove of spices, grains, and fresh produce, bringing the diverse flavors of India right to your doorstep. At Bhathal Indian Store, we pride ourselves on providing authentic ingredients that cater to all your culinary needs, whether you're preparing a traditional dish or exploring new recipes. Our shelves are stocked with a wide range of products, from essential pantry staples like rice and lentils to specialty items like ghee and pickles. We also offer a variety of fresh vegetables, herbs, and fruits, sourced directly from local farms and suppliers. Our knowledgeable and friendly staff are always ready to help you find exactly what you're looking for or to recommend new products to try. Whether you're a seasoned chef or new to Indian cooking, you'll find everything you need at Bhathal Indian Store to create delicious meals at home. Come and explore our store, where the essence of India meets the warmth of our community. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your culinary journey!
